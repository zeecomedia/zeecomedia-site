Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our international clients about working with Zeecomedia.

What time zones do you work in?

We're based in Nigeria (WAT/GMT+1) and maintain flexible hours to accommodate US, UK, and EU clients. We typically have 4-6 hours of overlap with US East Coast and full overlap with European time zones. We schedule regular meetings at times convenient for you.

How do you handle communication?

All our team members are fluent English speakers. We use Slack/Email for daily updates, Zoom/Google Meet for weekly video calls, and project management tools like Jira or Trello for transparency. You'll have direct access to your project manager and development team.

What are your payment terms?

We accept international payments via bank transfer, PayPal, Wise, or Payoneer. Typical payment structure: 40% upfront, 40% at midpoint milestone, 20% upon completion. For enterprise projects, we can discuss monthly billing or milestone-based payments. Escrow options available.

Do you sign NDAs and contracts?

Absolutely. We sign NDAs and provide formal contracts in English for all projects. We have standard agreements covering IP transfer, confidentiality, deliverables, timelines, and payment terms. We can also work with your legal team on custom contracts.

Who owns the code and intellectual property?

Upon final payment, you own 100% of the code, design assets, and intellectual property. We provide complete source code, documentation, and deployment credentials. This is standard in our contracts.

What happens after the project is completed?

Every project includes a 30-90 day warranty period for bug fixes. After that, we offer optional monthly maintenance retainers ($500-$2000/month) or hourly support ($50-$100/hour) for updates, features, and technical support. Many clients choose ongoing partnerships.

What if we're not satisfied with the work?

We work in iterative sprints with regular demos, so you see progress every week. If something isn't right, we revise it immediately - no questions asked. Our contracts include revision rounds at each milestone. Client satisfaction is our top priority, which is why we maintain 98% on-time delivery and high retention rates.

Can you help with ongoing development and scaling?

Yes! Many of our clients start with an MVP and continue working with us for years as their dedicated development partner. We can scale our team to match your growing needs, add new features, optimize performance, and help you expand into new markets.
